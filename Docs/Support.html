Routina Support

Need help with routines, notifications, widgets, Apple Watch sync, or privacy controls? Start here.

Contact

Email routina247@gmail.com with your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.

Common Help Topics

  • Notifications: confirm notification permission is enabled in iOS Settings and inside Routina settings.
  • Apple Watch: open Routina on iPhone first, then refresh the Watch app to request a fresh snapshot.
  • Widgets: if a widget looks stale, open Routina once to refresh local schedules and widget timelines.
  • Data: use Export my data before major device changes if you want a local backup.
