Routina Privacy Policy

Routina is designed around local-first routine tracking, private sync, and user-controlled data actions.

Information Routina Handles

Routina may store routine names, schedules, completion logs, notes, optional photos, settings, import/export files, and notification preferences that you create in the app.

Storage and Sync

Routine data is stored on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled, data syncs through your private iCloud database. Apple account and iCloud behavior are governed by Apple policies.

No Ads or Third-Party Analytics

Routina is planned as a paid app and does not rely on advertising, ad tracking, or third-party analytics SDKs.

Your Controls

The app direction includes data export, import, manual sync, erase all data, and privacy contact options. You can also manage notification and iCloud permissions in iOS Settings.

Photos

If you add photos to routines, Routina uses them only for the routine experience. Avoid adding sensitive images unless you are comfortable storing them on your device and in your enabled sync services.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@routina.app.
